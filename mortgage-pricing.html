COMMON QUESTIONS

Is there a long-term contract?

No. All plans are month-to-month. You can cancel anytime with 30 days' notice. We keep clients because of the results we deliver — not because of a contract.

How long does setup take?

Most loan officers are fully live within 5–7 business days of completing their onboarding form. Your website, automations, and dashboard are configured and handed off with a walkthrough call.

Does this work for independent brokers and retail LOs?

Yes. Whether you're an independent broker, a branch manager, or a producing LO at a mid-size lender, this system is built for you. Everything can be customized to your brand, NMLS number, and licensing footprint.

Can I upgrade plans later?

Absolutely. You can upgrade at any time and we'll prorate the difference. Most clients start on Originate or Growth, see results within 60–90 days, and upgrade from there.

What does the Realtor referral partner portal do?

The partner portal (included in Growth and Scale) gives your Realtor partners a simple co-branded page and update system — making it easy for them to refer business and stay in the loop on their client's loan. It's a tangible tool that helps you stand out when you're building referral relationships.

Are the websites compliant with NMLS requirements?

We build every mortgage website with standard NMLS disclosures, equal housing lender language, and licensing information. We're not your compliance officer — but we make sure the basics are in place and work with your NMLS details to keep everything accurate.
